The Science

More Energy

You will learn to focus on the things that matter and to let go of stressors that only cost you energy.

Better Sleep

People who practice cold exposure often report that their sleep quality is improved, and that they are feeling more energetic during the day.

Workout Recovery

Help your body to recover faster from physical exercise and reduce muscle soreness after intense workouts, by starting cold exposure.

Lower Blood Pressure

Cold Exposure Therapy helps limit stressors directly, and trains your blood vessals, leading to an overall drop in blood pressure.

Immunity Booster

If you're always tired, getting sick or any other immune downfalls? Cold Exposure helps teach your body how to handle invaders and lessen symptoms.

Boost Focus

With cold exposure, you will notice your concentration at levels you would have never imagined.

The Dive

Starting your journey with the cold is not an easy first step, since you are putting your body through a situation which is not necessarily comfortable at first. However the benefits are too good to miss, so punching through that first layer of ice is not too far ahead of you. Start with taking cold showers for a minute a day. If you want to challenge yourself more, change the shower to cold after taking a hot shower to provide a shock to the blood vessels and induce a responce from the body.
